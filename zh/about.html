    Research Overview

    My current research interests focus on quantum information and quantum optics theory in open systems. Firstly, I am studying the fundamentals of quantum information theory with Dr. Tomotaka Kuwahara, exploring the connection between entanglement entropy and quantum information processing. Now, we are looking for a universal relation between the operator spreading and information propagation. We are attempting to establish a limit for the Holevo capacity using the Lieb-Robinson bound [1]. Secondly, I am also researching the dynamical behavior of photon transmission [2] and the entanglement [3] of photon-phonon pairs based on a nano-optomechanical platform for applying quantum devices.
